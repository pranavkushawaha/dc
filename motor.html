About DC2 Motorcycles

DC Designs are one of the most wanted design and modification firm in India, founded and headed by the ace automotive designer Mr. Dilip Chabbria. The firm started as designing studio, which also builds concept and prototypes of different vehicles. They also builds custom vehicles on the special orders. DC Designs are the first designing firm to transform or customize high-end premium cars and SUVs according to the personal needs of customers. Not only the cars, they even transforms buses and light commercial passenger carrying vehicles into caravans and luxury vehicles with amenities. DC Avanti is the first production model from this group, is considered to be the cheapest sports car in the world with 2.0 litre, 4 cylinder, turbocharged engine inside a well built chassis and aerodynamically lowered body.

DC2 Motorcycles are the latest of all from this design house. It is the two-wheeler customization wing of DC Designs started with customizing Royal Enfield Classic model into a Carbon-Fibre bodied futuristic two-wheeler. Later they added Blue Admiral and Cardinal Red (Two new models) to their fleet. Enfield Carbon Shot is priced at Rs 75,000/ only. Many more concept motorcycles and prototypes are in pipeline.
