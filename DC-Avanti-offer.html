DC Avanti Latest Update

DC Avanti is a mid-engined sportscar made for India. It is powered by a 2.0-litre, four-cylinder turbo petrol engine which churns out 250PS@5,500rpm and a peak torque of 340Nm@2750rpm. This motor is coupled with a 6-speed manual transmission which feeds power to its rear wheels. The Avanti can clock 0-100kmph in 6 seconds on its way to an electronically limited top speed of 200kmph.
