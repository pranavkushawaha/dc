D C A V A N T I

Specifications Of DC Avanti

This supercar comes bolted with a 2000 cc Petrol engine. Sadly, it is available in Manual transmission only. Although supercars don’t have a direct relationship with a word called fuel economy, DC Avanti provides a good fuel efficiency of 10.8 kmpl.

Features Of DC Avanti

In exteriors, its look wow. It just attracts the surroundings eyeballs. Everything is designed to keep it as sporty as it could be. The front of the car comes loaded with dual projector headlamps and a strip of 5 LED DRLs. There’s a big black grille which helps in condensing the heat as well as enhances the spottiness of the Avanti.

Look side, you get sharp curve lines which go throughout the end of the car. Also, there’s big side air intake in order to meet good aerodynamics. The supercar runs on these big 5 dual-spoke Alloy wheels which looks nice.

Come to the rear, you will get stunned. There are lots of things to discuss. You get a floating roof design which goes to the rear end.The twin triangular shaped exhaust pipe looks well-proportioned with overall profile. The centre has a honeycomb textured grille which holds DC badging and empty space for the number plate.

Modification Price Of DC Avanti

The price of DC Avanti starts from 48.0 lakh. You can customise the supercar according to your needs.
